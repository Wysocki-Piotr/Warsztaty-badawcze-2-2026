# How can we model logical relations in latent space?

The core of our course is to verify the CAVs for concepts that are hierarchical $A \rightarrow B$. In terms of logic, we can describe it as implication, as the existence of $A$ implies the existence of $B$. This is, however, just an example, and other relations can be modelled as well. Let's consider two concepts that are independent of each other ($A\not\models B$, $B\not\models A$). We could postulate that $SV(A) \perp SV(B)$. Further examples are "OR", "IF AND ONLY IF", and "XOR". **This project aims to find these relations in real data by proposing relation detection methods. Then, analysis of existing and new CAVs should be performed on these relations.**
